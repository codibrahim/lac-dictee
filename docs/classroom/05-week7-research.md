**Due: May 11**

---

## Objective

Learn to sell the Impact, not just the Code. Prepare a high-impact presentation.

---

## Instructions

- **Presentation Storytelling:** Pitch the "Impact" (time/money saved), not just the code.
  - *From:* "I wrote this function"
  - *To:* "I saved teachers 6 hours a week"
- **UX Polish:** "Don't Make Me Think" principles for AI interfaces.
- **Data Storytelling:** Use metrics (time saved, error reduction) to prove value.
