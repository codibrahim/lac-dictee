# Research & Learning Materials: The Founder's Toolkit

**Source:** [Google Classroom](https://classroom.google.com/c/ODQ1MDc1NTAxMjM1/a/ODYwNTUwNTMxNTA3/details)
**Due: Apr 27**

---

## Objective

Shift from "Team Member" to "Problem Solver." Build a foundation in lean business modeling and technical scoping.

---

## Instructions

- **The Lean Mindset:** Understand how to validate a business idea before writing a single line of code.
- **SMART Goals:** Example: *"By Friday, I will have a technical blueprint that justifies why Python is the best tool for my specific problem."*

---

## Content Areas

### The Strategy Layer: Lean Startup Basics
- Lean Canvas: How to map a business model on one page
- Problem Statements: Translating a "grumble" into a solvable technical gap

### The Logic Layer: Tech Stack Evaluation
- Build vs. Buy: When to use an API, custom Python, or No-Code (Zapier/Make)
- Design Sprints: Understand → Sketch → Decide → Prototype → Validate

### The Future Layer: AI Agency
- Autonomous Workflows: How AI can trigger actions without human intervention
